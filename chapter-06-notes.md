# Chapter 6 — Protecting the Consumer Web

## Abuse domains
- Account takeover
- Fake account creation
- Financial fraud
- Bot activity

## Feature families
- Velocity: rates, bursts, error ratios
- Reputation: entity history and trust
- Behavioral consistency: device/time/geo/flow

## Operational strategy
Use multi-stage responses: allow, challenge (CAPTCHA/MFA), or block.
